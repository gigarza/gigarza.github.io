ABOUT

Georgina Garza is currently a senior at The University of Texas at Austin, pursuing the simultaneous majors of B.A. Dance and B.S.A. Computer Science. While at UT, she has performed as part of Dance Repertory Theatre in Fall For Dance (2018, 2019), Fortitude (2019), and unstaged (R)evolution (2020), with works by Rena Butler, Erica Gionfriddo, Charles O. Anderson, and more. Prior, she traveled with Diverse Space Youth Dance Theatre, under the direction of Toni Bravo, to perform in the Irish Youth Dance Festival. Locally in Austin, Texas, Georgina was a part of The Andrea Torres Dance Company and Visions in Rhythm, dancing at local venues.
